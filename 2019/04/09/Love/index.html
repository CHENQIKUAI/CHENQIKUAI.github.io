爱情 众水不能熄灭

Love

2019-04-09

Many waters cannot quench love, Neither do the floods drown it: Even if a man gave all the substance of his house for love, It would utterly be contemned.

爱情，众水不能熄灭，
大水也不能淹没，
若有人拿家中所有的财宝要换爱情，
就全被藐视。
